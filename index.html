I am a Senior Research Scientist and Investigator at A*STAR, Singapore. I received my Ph.D. degree from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2017. My research focuses on graph and geometric deep learning, foundation models, and AI for scientific discovery.

My current research develops structure-aware and data-efficient learning methods for foundation models, graph intelligence, and scientific discovery.
