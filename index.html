I am a Senior Research Scientist and Investigator at A*STAR, Singapore. I received my Ph.D. degree from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2017. My research focuses on graph and geometric deep learning, foundation models, and AI for scientific discovery.

My current research develops structure-aware and data-efficient learning methods for foundation models, graph intelligence, and scientific discovery.

Job Opening

  • Postdoctoral research fellow
    • One postdoctoral position is available in my group. This position is fully supported by research projects at the intersection of advanced machine learning and scientific applications. If your research interests lie in one or several of the following: Graph and geometric deep learning, Federated learning, Spatio-temporal and multi-modal learning, Foundation model fine-tuning and reasoning, and applications in Materials science and Catalyst discovery, Weather modeling, Lightning nowcasting, and Environmental risk forecasting, and you have a strong publication record in these fields, you are welcome to apply for these positions by sending me an email, attaching your CV.

    Exchange student, Student intern, and Visiting scholar
    We are always seeking PhD students and researchers who want to further explore their research through short-term visits supported by national programs (e.g., CSC in China) or research projects (mine or those from your lab). If you work on one of the following areas: Graph and geometric deep learning, Federated learning, Spatio-temporal and multi-modal learning, Foundation model fine-tuning and reasoning, and are interested in joining my group, you are welcome to nominate me to host your visit by sending me an email, attaching your CV and a brief research statement.
