Dr. Tiantian He

Center for Frontier AI Research (CFAR), Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC), Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR)

I'm a Senior Research Scientist and Early Career Investigator with the Center for Frontier AI Research (CFAR), Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC), Singapore Institute of Manufacturing Technology (SIMTech), Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR). I obtained my PhD degree from Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2017. My research interests include AI, Computational Intelligence, Graph Deep Learning, Data-Centric Transfer Optimization, and Data Mining.
